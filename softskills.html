Soft Skills: Teaching & Student Reviews

Physics Instructional Assistent

I have been an instructional assistent at UC San Diego for the past two years. I have been an instructional for five unique courses. To learn more about my teaching style and soft skills check out a few of my student reviews below.
** All reviews were submitted anonomously at the end of each quarter full evaluation reports can be requested from UCSD's evaluation system (https://cape.ucsd.edu/).

Waves, Optics & Modern Physics - Spring 2021

Supervisor: Professor Kam S Arnold

"Courtney answers student's questions effectively and clearly. She helped my understanding of the course material a lot. She guides students to the correct answer. She is always willing to clarify if something is not clear. She is committed to helping students understand the course material."
"Courtney is really passionate and makes going to discussion more engaging."
"Courtney was such a big help in this class!! She really furthered my understanding of the lecture material and homework questions. She was always willing to answer my questions and provide me with clarification. I genuinely would have struggled a lot more in this course if it weren't for Courtney's weekly problem sessions."
"I was unable to attend the "TA sessions" that were available to us but the meetings were recorded and Courtney's explanation for the homework/concept questions that students had were clear and helpful. She also replied to emails/messages quickly when asked. Being one of two TAs for a class that had like 900 students, I think she did an exceptional job." "Courtney really did such an amazing job explaining homework questions clearly. I understood things so much better after listening to her explanations, and she genuinely always wanted to help us students. She was always patient!"
"Courtney was a great TA for both Phys1B and Phys1C and I really learned a great deal from her!"

Electricity and Magnetism - Winter 2021

Supervisor: Professor Alexander Groisman

"Her notes were great. I read through them before tests. They were an excellent recap before tests."
"Courtney actually helped me learn the material in this course. She explained concepts really well and lead great problems sessions."
"Courtney is such a kind and caring person. She is great at answering questions and is willing to go through a concept multiple times until we all understand it. You can truly tell how passionate Courtney is about Physics and she always has a smile on her face."
"Courtney makes difficult concepts a lot easier to understand by providing multiple examples and explaining it until we all understand in office hours. She is the best TA I have ever had and makes the course material exciting and interesting."
